3 Population deviation is the range between the most populous and least population districts, divided by the ideal district size.
4 The ratio of each plan's total perimeter from all districts, divided by the total perimeter from the shortest plan. A value of 1 indicates the most compact plan.
5 The modelled lean of the 17th seat in the 2022 State Senate elections, modelled using results from statewide races.
